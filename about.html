My Story

Prior to studying User Experience Design at General Assembly, I was a Marketing Planning Analyst at LG Electronics Mobile. I realized there was something special about a good user experience that increased brand awareness and loyalty. I wanted to pursue a career where I could directly focus on understanding the user, and creating a product based around the users' needs.

It just so happens to be that this pursuit is complimented by my BA in Psychology & minor in Digital Arts.
